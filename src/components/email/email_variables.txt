TEMPLATE VARIABLES FOR BOOKING CONFIRMATION EMAIL
===================================================

Use these EXACT variable names in your email template in the database:

CUSTOMER & PARTNER:
  {{customer_name}}        - Full name of the customer (e.g., "Mario Rossi")
  {{partner_name}}         - Company/partner name (e.g., "MLM GmbH")

BOOKING DETAILS:
  {{booking_date}}         - Formatted date (e.g., "mercoledì 19 novembre 2025")
  {{resource}}             - Resource name (e.g., "Hot Desk A1")
  {{location_name}}        - Location name (e.g., "Milano Centro")

CONTRACT INFO:
  {{contract_number}}      - Contract number (e.g., "MLM-20250119-001")
  {{service_name}}         - Service name (e.g., "Pacchetto 10 entrate")

DURATION:
  {{duration_type}}        - "Giornata intera" or "Mezza giornata"
  {{time_slot}}            - If half day: "Mattina (9:00 - 13:00)" or "Pomeriggio (14:00 - 18:00)"

ENTRIES:
  {{entries_used}}         - Entries consumed by this booking (1 or 0.5)
  {{remaining_count}}      - Entries remaining after this booking

EXAMPLE TEMPLATE:
=================

Ciao {{customer_name}},

Ottima notizia! La tua prenotazione è stata confermata con successo.

Dettagli Prenotazione:
- Servizio: {{service_name}}
- Data: {{booking_date}}
- Risorsa: {{resource}}
- Ingressi Rimanenti: {{remaining_count}}

Non vediamo l'ora di vederti!

Cordiali saluti,
Il Tuo Team {{partner_name}}
